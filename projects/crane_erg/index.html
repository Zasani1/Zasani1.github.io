Abstract

This project presents a constrained control framework for cooperative manipulation of heavy payloads using a heterogeneous dual-robot system composed of a 7-DoF Franka Emika Panda manipulator and a 2-DoF overhead crane. The proposed approach integrates the Explicit Reference Governor (ERG) with a pre-stabilized control scheme to ensure constraint satisfaction without online optimization.

A trajectory-based ERG formulation, combining a Dynamic Safety Margin (DSM) and a Navigation Field (NF), dynamically adjusts the reference trajectory to guarantee safe and coordinated motion between the two robots. Experimental results confirm that the proposed scheme enables the manipulator to handle payloads several times heavier than its nominal capacity while maintaining safe, constraint-compliant cooperation.
