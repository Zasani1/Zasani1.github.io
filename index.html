About

I am a researcher at the Université Libre de Bruxelles (ULB) and Vrije Universiteit Brussel (VUB), working on advanced control of cooperative and constrained robotic systems. My research focuses on integrating Explicit Reference Governors (ERG), constraint handling, and real-time control for heterogeneous multi-robot systems.

Recent Projects

  • Crane–Robot Cooperative Control Framework — combining a 7-DoF Franka arm and a 2-DoF overhead crane for heavy-load manipulation using ERG control.
  • Dual-Arm ERG + RelaxedIK Integration — developing a multi-arm closed-chain controller with constraint-aware inverse kinematics (work in progress).
